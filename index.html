Treebrick

Tension. Struggle. Symbiosis? The intoxicating contrast of organic and angular forms! The return of nature, or its inevitable demise? The loneliness of untranslatable words... The vertigo of living at the bleeding edge of history...

We are overcome by these thoughts and questions when we find ourselves in the vicinity of Chicago's greatest (preter)natural wonder: treebrick. This website is an attempt to celebrate not only treebrick, but also the surprising beauty of everyday things that it so well embodies. Thank you for visiting.
