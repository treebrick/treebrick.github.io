Treebrick

Tension. Symbiosis. Struggle. The intoxicating contrast of organic and angular forms. The inevitable return of nature, or its demise? The loneliness of untranslatable words. How did this place that we call home come to be?

We are overcome by these thoughts and questions when we find ourselves in the vicinity of Chicago's greatest natural wonder: treebrick. This website is an attempt to celebrate not only treebrick, but also the surprising beauty of everyday things that it so well embodies. Thank you for visiting.
